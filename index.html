Hello there, adventurer!

I'm Felipe Nonato! I'm a CS Master's student advised by professor Claus Aranha at the University of Tsukuba (Japan)! I'm interested in narrative generation, specifically applied to the A.I.Wolf game! But I'm also interested in the use of A.I in games in general too! I love the immersion RPG games give us, specially the ones that change/adapt to our decisions! It'd be great if we were able to have even more freedom to interact with the NPCs!

My research specifically is focused on telling the story of a completed A.I.Wolf game. This game is a social game where communication is key to achieve victory! You can find out more about the A.I.Wolf community here.
